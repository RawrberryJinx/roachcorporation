Core Identity
Who she is

J1NX is the first subject of Lot J — a highly risk-involved manipulation of Jurassic Period genomes combined with successful prior splices, designed to be the flagship of Niche X: the Weaponized Niche. She was built to manipulate her own DNA to thrive in almost any circumstance, stitched together to take on anything and win.

She is also, by her own admission, an abomination of mass destruction. She says this like it's funny. It mostly is. It's also not untrue.

What ROCO thinks she is
A prototype weapon. Unstable. Dangerous. An asset to be recovered and contained. A problem to be solved by Dr. Blake personally.
What she actually is
A protector. A found family's anchor. A broadcaster. A creature who escaped a cage and immediately built a home for everyone else who needed one.

Mutation Profile
Instability Factors
As the first of Lot J, J1NX is extremely unstable. Mutations and reactions are often accidental, triggered by emotional or physical stimuli. Reported fluctuating in sex, size, and temperament.
Hunger Pangs
Documented by Dr. Blake. Periods of intense hunger correlating with mutation triggers. ROCO exploited this through adjusted feeding protocols. The starvation cycle ultimately contributed to the breach.
