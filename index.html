About

Science at the
edge of what is possible.

Roach Corporation was founded on a single premise: that the boundaries of biology are not fixed. They are negotiable.

We operate at the intersection of genomic research, behavioral science, and advanced materials — pushing the limits of what living systems can become. Our work is not published. Our results speak for themselves.

We do not seek recognition. We seek outcomes. The organisms, systems, and solutions we develop are built to last — to adapt, to endure, and to outlast every alternative.

Ethical Frameworks
Roach Corporation operates within all applicable legal and ethical frameworks across all of its departments, in all jurisdictions in which we operate. We do not comment further on internal research protocols.

Research Divisions

What we can share.

Not all Roach Corporation research programs are disclosed publicly. Proprietary and classified programs are protected under standing contractual and legal frameworks. We thank you for your understanding.

Division 01
Advanced Genomics
Research into genome sequencing, manipulation, and expression across a broad range of biological subjects. Our genomics division has been operational since the founding of the corporation and remains our most productive research unit.
Life Sciences
Division 02
Behavioral Analytics
Applied research into behavioral prediction, pattern modeling, and compliance optimization. Our analytics programs utilize proprietary AI systems developed in-house. Results are not published externally.
Applied Science
Division 03
Adaptive Materials
Investigation into biological and synthetic materials capable of autonomous adaptation and response. This division works in close collaboration with our genomics team on subjects requiring materials-level intervention.
Materials Research
Division 04
Extraplanar Studies
A specialized research unit investigating methods of biological enhancement through non-conventional means. This division operates under separate oversight protocols and with limited external disclosure.
Occult Research
Division 05
Containment Sciences
Research and operational support for the management of biological subjects, programs, and events requiring specialized containment protocols. This division liaises directly with all other research units.
